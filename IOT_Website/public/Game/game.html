Snake Game:Instructions

The player controls a dot, square, or object on a bordered plane. As it moves forward, it leaves a trail behind, resembling a moving snake. The player loses when the snake runs into the screen border, other obstacle, or itself.

Tetris Game:Instructions

The objective of the game is to use the pieces to create as many horizontal lines of blocks as possible. When a line is completed, it disappears, and the blocks placed above fall one rank. .

Tic Tac Toe:Instructions

The first player, who shall be designated "X", has three possible strategically distinct positions to mark during the first turn. The second player, who shall be designated "O", must respond to X's opening mark in such a way as to avoid the forced win.

Soduko Game:Instructions

Line up the rows and columns. One basic rule of the game is every column and row must have all of the numbers from 1 to 9. Pay attention to the numbers in the large squares. Similarly, in each of the 9 large squares, every number from 1 to 9 must appear.
